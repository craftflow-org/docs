## Setting up an integration

Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the CraftFlow web app. Click the integration you want to set up and follow the on-screen steps. Most integrations use OAuth and are connected in under 5 minutes.

<Note>
  You'll need admin-level access in both CraftFlow and the platform you're connecting to complete the OAuth flow.
</Note>

## What syncs

The exact data that syncs depends on the integration, but typically includes:

- **Customers** — Name, address, contact info, service history
- **Jobs** — Job type, status, assigned technician, scheduled time
- **Technicians** — Names and availability for dispatching
- **Business units** — For companies with multiple locations or divisions

## Webhooks

CraftFlow can also receive data from external systems via webhook. This is useful if your software isn't listed above but supports outbound webhooks. Contact [support@craftflow.com](mailto:support@craftflow.com) for setup help.
