---
title: "HubSpot Integration"
description: "Connect CraftFlow to HubSpot to sync contacts and drive sales follow-up."
---

The HubSpot integration syncs customer contact data between CraftFlow and HubSpot, letting your sales team track leads and follow-up opportunities alongside your service data.

## What syncs

| Data | Direction | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Contacts | Bidirectional | Customer info kept in sync between both platforms |
| Deals | HubSpot → CraftFlow | Sales pipeline data surfaced in CraftFlow |
| Lead form submissions | HubSpot → CraftFlow | Web form leads routed into CraftFlow workflows |

## Connecting HubSpot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In CraftFlow, go to **Settings → Integrations** and click **HubSpot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to HubSpot">
    You'll be redirected to HubSpot to authorize the connection. Sign in with your HubSpot admin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize CraftFlow">
    Review the permissions CraftFlow is requesting and click **Authorize**. CraftFlow needs read/write access to contacts and deals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure sync settings">
    Choose which HubSpot properties map to CraftFlow fields, and set up any filtering rules (e.g., only sync contacts in a specific pipeline stage).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lead routing

If you're using HubSpot forms on your website, CraftFlow can receive new form submissions as leads and route them to your inside sales team for follow-up. Go to **Inside Sales → AI Agent Campaigns** to configure automated follow-up sequences for new HubSpot leads.

## Disconnecting

To disconnect HubSpot, go to **Settings → Integrations → HubSpot** and click **Disconnect**. This stops the sync but doesn't delete any data already in CraftFlow.
