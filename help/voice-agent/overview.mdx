CraftFlow's **AI Voice Agent** answers inbound calls when your team can't — after hours, during peak volume, or as a first-line filter before routing to a live dispatcher. It speaks naturally, understands customer requests, and can book appointments directly into your dispatch board.

## What the voice agent can do

- Answer inbound calls immediately with no hold time
- Collect customer name, address, and issue description
- Check availability and book appointments into ServiceTitan (or your connected CRM)
- Handle common questions (service area, pricing ranges, hours)
- Transfer calls to a live agent when needed
- Send confirmation texts to customers after booking

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer calls your number">
    Your main business number (or an after-hours number) routes to the CraftFlow Voice Agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI handles the conversation">
    The voice agent greets the customer, asks about their issue, checks your availability, and books the job if everything qualifies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Job is created in your system">
    A new job is created in your connected field management software (e.g., ServiceTitan) with the customer's details and appointment time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You review the call">
    Every voice agent call is recorded and available in CraftFlow for review. You'll see a transcript and summary of what the AI did.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuring the voice agent

Go to **Settings → Voice Agent** to configure:

- **Greeting** — Customize how the agent introduces itself and your company
- **Service areas** — Define the ZIP codes or cities you serve. Callers outside your area are handled gracefully.
- **Business hours** — Set when the agent should be active vs. routing to live staff
- **Job types** — Which types of jobs the agent can book autonomously
- **Transfer rules** — When and where to transfer to a live person (e.g., emergency calls, complex jobs)
- **Membership programs** — Let the agent mention and offer your maintenance agreements

## Call transfer rules

You can configure the agent to transfer calls under specific conditions:

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Customer says it's an emergency | Transfer immediately to on-call dispatcher |
| Job type not supported for online booking | Transfer to CSR |
| Customer requests to speak to a person | Transfer to CSR |
| Outside service area | Politely decline and end call |

<Note>
  The voice agent is designed to handle the majority of after-hours and overflow calls. Complex situations and edge cases are always handed off to your team.
</Note>

## Reviewing voice agent calls

All calls handled by the voice agent appear in **Call Center AI → Calls**, tagged as Voice Agent calls. You can review the transcript, hear the audio, and see how the booking was handled.
