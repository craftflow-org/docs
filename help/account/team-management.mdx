---
title: "Team Management"
description: "Add team members, assign roles, and manage access in CraftFlow."
---

Go to **Settings → Team** to manage who has access to your CraftFlow account and what they can do.

## Inviting team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Click your account name in the top-left, then select **Settings**, and navigate to the **Team** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Invite Member'">
    Enter the team member's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a role">
    Choose the appropriate role for this person (see role descriptions below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    The team member will receive an email with a link to set up their account. Invites expire after 7 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage settings, integrations, billing, and all team members |
| **Manager** | View and review all calls and recordings, assign coaching, manage scorecards |
| **CSR** | View their own call scores and coaching assignments |
| **Technician** | Record calls on mobile, view their own recordings and feedback |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to calls and recordings — useful for owners who want visibility without editing anything |

<Note>
  Technicians primarily use the mobile app. They don't need web app access unless they want to review their own recordings in more detail.
</Note>

## Removing a team member

Go to **Settings → Team**, find the member, and click the **...** menu next to their name. Select **Remove**. Their data (recordings, calls) will remain in CraftFlow — only their login access is revoked.

## Resending an invite

If a team member didn't receive their invite or it expired, go to **Settings → Team**, find their pending invite, and click **Resend**.
