---

## Overview

When a customer messages your business through a Google Local Services Ad, Craft picks up the lead and has your AI messaging agent respond — typically within a couple of minutes. The conversation happens entirely within Google's messaging platform.

This works with **message-type leads** (not phone calls). Your AI agent handles the conversation just like it does on SMS, Messenger, and other channels.

---

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Google LSA settings">
    Go to [**Call Center AI → Speed to Lead → Google LSA**](https://app.craftflow.co/call-center-ai/ai-csr/speed-to-lead/google-lsa) and click **Connect Google Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google">
    Sign in with the Google account that has access to your Google Ads manager account. Grant Craft permission to manage your Local Services Ads conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your LSA accounts">
    Choose which LSA accounts you want the AI agent to handle. Each account corresponds to a business location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

New conversations will appear under [**Call Center AI → Tasks**](https://app.craftflow.co/call-center-ai/tasks) with the Google LSA channel icon. You can filter by channel to see only LSA conversations.
