Craft is an AI-powered sales coaching platform built for home services companies. We help your team sell more, book more, and recover more revenue — without adding headcount.

## Three Products, One Platform

### In-Home Sales AI

**For: Sales reps and techs running in-home appointments**

Craft listens to your in-home sales conversations and coaches your reps to sell better — in real time.

- **Virtual ride-alongs** — AI listens to every appointment, no manager needed
- **Real-time coaching (Checkpoint)** — Reps get AI tips mid-appointment during natural pauses
- **Process scorecards** — Every appointment scored against your sales process (0-5 per step)
- **Post-appointment summaries** — AI highlights what went well and what to improve
- **Personality insights** — AI reads the customer's communication style to help reps adapt

Works on any iPhone, iPad, or Android device. One tap to start.

### Contact Center AI

**For: CSRs, booking agents, and call center managers**

Everything your contact center needs to book more appointments — agent coaching, AI voice, and instant lead response.

- **Agent Coach & QA** — Every call scored against your booking script with specific coaching feedback
- **Booking rate tracking** — See which reps book and which don't, and why
- **Spam filtering** — AI separates real customer calls from junk
- **Speed to Lead** — Instant AI response to web, SMS, and ad-generated leads so you never lose a lead to slow response times
- **AI Voice Agent** — 24/7 call answering, web chat, and SMS booking when your team is offline

Connects to your existing phone system (RingCentral, Five9, etc.). No hardware needed.

### Follow Up

**For: Inside sales reps, rehash managers, and field reps following up on unsold estimates**

Craft takes the context your team already captures on every appointment — what was quoted, objections raised, customer personality type, customer sentiment — and turns it into personalized, AI-driven follow-up at scale.

- **Recovering unsold estimates** — Craft identifies open opportunities from completed appointments and re-engages homeowners via SMS and email, using the customer's why, what was actually quoted, objections raised, and customer sentiment
- **Arming your ISRs** — Inside sales reps get a co-pilot that shows exactly what happened on the call, so they know what to say on the follow-up. No guessing.
- **Re-engaging cold leads** — AI Rehash agents reference the original conversation to bring dead leads back to life, arming your field reps with an AI co-pilot that helps them rehash leads
