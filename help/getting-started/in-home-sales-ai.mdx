Get set up and recording your first appointment in under 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Download the App

- **iPhone/iPad**: Search "Craft AI" in the App Store
- **Android**: Search "Craft AI" in the Google Play Store

Download and open the app. Log in with the email and password from your invite.

<Note>
Don't have login credentials? Ask your manager or admin to send you an invite from Settings > Users in the Craft dashboard.
</Note>

## Step 2: Allow Permissions

When the app asks, tap **Allow** for:

- **Microphone** — required for recording (this is the most important one)
- **Notifications** — so you get reminders to record at appointments
- **Location** (optional) — helps auto-match recordings to jobs in your CRM

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone/iPad">
    If you accidentally denied microphone access: Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > find Craft > toggle ON
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    If you accidentally denied microphone access: Settings > Apps > Craft > Permissions > Microphone > "Allow only while using the app"
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Record Your First Appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Craft app when you arrive at the job" />
  <Step title="Tap the Record button" />
  <Step title="Confirm or select the customer/job" />
  <Step title="Place your device in your shirt pocket, microphone facing up" />
  <Step title="Run your appointment as normal" />
  <Step title="When you're done, tap Stop" />
  <Step title="Wait for the green checkmark">
    This means your recording uploaded successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your recording will be scored and ready to review within minutes.

## Quick Tips for Great Recordings

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Shirt pocket, mic facing up | Face-down on a table |
| Within 3 feet of the conversation | Across the room |
| 20%+ battery before starting | Start with low battery |
| Remove thick/magnetic cases | Leave a bulky case on |

<Tip>
Do a 10-second test recording before your first real appointment. Play it back. If you can hear yourself clearly, you're good to go.
</Tip>

## Using Checkpoint (Real-Time AI Coaching)

Checkpoint gives you AI coaching during the appointment — not after.

**How to use it:**
1. While recording, tap the **Checkpoint** icon
2. AI analyzes your conversation so far
3. You get instant tips: what to cover next, how the customer is responding, objection suggestions

**Best times to use Checkpoint:**
- After your inspection, before presenting options
- When the customer is reviewing paperwork
- During any natural pause

It takes 2 seconds to glance at. Customers won't notice.

## After Your Appointment

Your recording is automatically:
- **Transcribed** — full text of the conversation
- **Scored** — each step of your sales process rated 0-5
- **Summarized** — AI highlights 2 things you did well and 1 area to improve

Find it all in your **Library** (bottom menu of the app).

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I don't have cell service at the job?">
    No problem. Craft records offline and uploads automatically when you reconnect to WiFi or cell signal. Just keep the app open until you see the green checkmark.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can my manager hear my recordings?">
    Yes — managers and admins can review recordings and scores. That's how they coach you. Think of it like a virtual ride-along. You can also review your own recordings and scores anytime.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I forget to record?">
    Build the habit: open Craft when you arrive, hit Record before you knock on the door. Some teams set up automatic reminders through dispatch notifications.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does it drain my battery?">
    Minimal impact. A typical 1-2 hour appointment uses about 5-10% battery. Start with 20%+ and you'll be fine.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I pause the recording?">
    Yes. Tap Pause for phone calls or private moments. The app also auto-pauses for incoming calls. Tap Resume to continue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the DISC personality thing?">
    Craft analyzes the customer's communication style during the conversation and tells you their personality type (Dominant, Influential, Steady, or Conscientious). This helps you adjust your approach. You'll see this in Checkpoint.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
