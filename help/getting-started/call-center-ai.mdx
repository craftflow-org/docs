## How Call Center AI Works

Unlike In-Home Sales AI (which uses a mobile app), Call Center AI connects directly to your phone system. Once connected, it automatically listens to and scores every inbound call. Your CSRs don't need to download anything or change how they work.

**What happens automatically:**
1. Customer calls your main number
2. Craft AI listens to the call in real time
3. Call is scored against your booking script
4. CSR and manager get coaching feedback after the call
5. Qualified calls that didn't book are flagged for follow-up

## Step 2: Build Your Call Scorecard

Your scorecard defines what a great call looks like. We'll help you set this up during onboarding, but here's what a typical booking script scorecard includes:

- **Greeting** — Did the CSR answer with the company greeting?
- **Caller identification** — Did they get the customer's name and contact info?
- **Need identification** — Did they ask the right questions about the service needed?
- **Availability & booking** — Did they offer appointment times and confirm the booking?
- **Objection handling** — If the customer hesitated, did the CSR address their concern?
- **Closing & confirmation** — Did they confirm the appointment details and set expectations?

Your scorecard is fully customizable to match your process.

## Step 3: Review Calls in the Dashboard

Once connected, your dashboard shows:

- **Every call scored** — see which CSRs are following the script and which need help
- **Booking rate by rep** — who books and who doesn't, and why
- **Qualified vs. junk calls** — AI separates real customer calls from spam, vendors, and existing customer check-ins
- **Coaching opportunities** — click any low-scoring call to see exactly where the CSR went off-script

Never miss another call. Craft's AI Voice Agent answers calls, texts, and web chats when your team is unavailable.

**What it does:**
- Answers calls 24/7 with your company's greeting and brand voice

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do my CSRs need to do anything differently?">
    No. Call Center AI works in the background. CSRs answer calls the same way they always have. They'll just get coaching feedback after each call.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I score outbound calls too?">
    Yes. If your phone system records outbound calls, Craft can score those as well.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How fast do scores appear?">
    Call scores are typically available within minutes of the call ending.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a call is spam or not a real customer?">
    Craft's AI automatically identifies and filters spam, vendor, and non-customer calls. These won't affect your team's booking rate metrics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can different CSR teams have different scorecards?">
    Yes. You can create separate scorecards for different teams, departments, or call types (e.g., booking calls vs. service calls vs. sales calls).
