AI Ridealong is your AI coach in the field. It records and transcribes every in-person customer conversation, delivers real-time coaching mid-appointment, and gives you the insights to win more.

## What You Get

### Real-Time AI Coaching

Checkpoint gives you AI feedback during the appointment — not after. During natural pauses, glance at your phone for tips on what to cover next, how to handle objections, and when to close. Customers won't notice.

### Conversation Analytics

Every recording is analyzed for:

- **Talk time & talk ratio** — are you listening enough or talking too much?
- **Talk speed** — too fast loses trust, too slow loses attention
- **Filler word usage** — "um", "uh", "like" tracked so you can clean up your delivery
- **Questions asked** — are you running discovery or just presenting?
- **Patience** — are you letting the customer finish before responding?

### Process Scorecards

Every appointment scored 0-5 against your team's sales process. See exactly which steps you nailed and which need work. Managers see the same scores and can leave coaching comments on specific moments.

### Post-Appointment Insights

After every recording:
- Full transcript — searchable, shareable
- AI summary — what went well, what to improve
- Customer personality type (DISC) — adapt your approach next time
