---
title: "Call Recording & Analysis"
description: "Record, transcribe, and analyze field calls from your technicians — then use the insights to coach and improve performance."
---

CraftFlow's call recording feature (called **AI Ridealong**) lets managers listen in on real field calls without physically being there. Every recorded call is automatically transcribed, analyzed by AI, and scored against your team's custom criteria.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Technician records the call">
    The tech opens the CraftFlow mobile app and taps record before a customer call or at the start of a service appointment conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="CraftFlow processes the recording">
    After the call ends, the audio is uploaded and processed. Within a few minutes you'll have a full transcript, AI summary, and tracker scores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manager reviews the analysis">
    Managers and coaches log into the web app to review recordings, read the AI analysis, and leave coaching notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Coaching is assigned">
    If a call needs follow-up, a manager can create a coaching assignment directly from the recording and assign it to the tech.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automatic transcription" icon="file-lines">
    Every call is transcribed word-for-word so you can search, skim, and review without listening to the full audio.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI trackers" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Set up custom trackers to detect phrases, objections, or moments in calls — like whether the tech offered a membership or asked about the equipment age.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Key moments" icon="flag">
    AI automatically flags important moments in the conversation so reviewers can jump straight to the relevant parts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Coaching assignments" icon="chalkboard-teacher">
    Create structured coaching tasks from any recording and assign them to technicians with due dates and notes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
