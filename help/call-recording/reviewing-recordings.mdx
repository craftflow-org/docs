---
title: "Reviewing Recordings"
description: "How managers and coaches review field call recordings, read AI analysis, and assign coaching."
---

Once a call has been recorded and processed, managers can review it in the CraftFlow web app. Each recording includes a transcript, AI-generated summary, tracker results, and key moments.

## Finding recordings

Navigate to **AI Ridealong → Recordings** in the sidebar. You can filter by:

- **Technician** — see recordings from a specific team member
- **Date range** — narrow down by when the call happened
- **Folder** — if your team organizes recordings into folders by job type or region
- **Tracker results** — filter for calls where a specific tracker fired (or didn't)

## What's in a recording

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Transcript" icon="file-lines">
    A word-for-word transcript of the call with speaker labels. Click any line to jump to that moment in the audio.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Summary" icon="sparkles">
    A short AI-generated summary of what happened on the call — useful for quickly understanding the context before diving deeper.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tracker results" icon="list-check">
    Shows which of your custom trackers fired during the call. Green = detected, Red = not detected.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Key moments" icon="flag">
    AI-flagged moments worth reviewing — objections, price discussions, opportunities, etc.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Leaving feedback and assigning coaching

You can leave timestamped comments anywhere in the transcript. To assign a coaching task:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recording">
    Click the recording you want to review from the recordings list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Assign Coaching'">
    Use the button in the top-right of the recording view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the coaching form">
    Add a title, description, and due date. The tech will be notified and can complete the assignment in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organizing recordings into folders

Folders help you keep recordings organized by job type, technician, or training program. Create folders under **AI Ridealong → Folders** and drag recordings in, or assign a folder at upload time.

## Setting up trackers

Trackers are keywords or phrases you want the AI to listen for. Go to **AI Ridealong → Trackers** to create and manage them.

Common examples:
- **Membership offer** — detects if the tech offered a maintenance agreement
- **Price objection** — detects if the customer pushed back on price
- **Equipment age** — detects if the tech asked about the age of the system
