## How Scoring Works

Each scorecard item is scored 0-5 by the AI based on what was said during the conversation:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Fully executed |
| 4 | Mostly there |
| 3 | Adequate |
| 2 | Partial |
| 1 | Barely addressed |
| 0 | Not addressed |
| N/A | Didn't apply |

Scores are weighted — high-impact steps like options presentation and objection handling can carry more weight than lower-impact steps. Items marked N/A are excluded entirely. See [Scorecard Weights](/help/getting-started/scorecard-weights) for the math.

## Coaching Comments

Managers can leave comments on specific moments in any recording. Reps see these in their app.

**Good comments are:**
- Specific — reference the exact moment
- Balanced — note wins alongside areas to improve
- Actionable — tell them what to do differently
- Brief — 1-2 sentences
