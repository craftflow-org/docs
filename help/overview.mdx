## What is CraftFlow?

CraftFlow is a platform built specifically for home service companies — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and related trades. It gives owners, managers, and coaches the tools to:

- **Record and analyze field calls** so you can coach technicians on real conversations, not role-plays.
- **Score every inbound call** automatically so no booking opportunity slips through the cracks.
- **Deploy an AI voice agent** that answers after-hours calls, books appointments, and handles basic customer questions without a dispatcher.
- **Connect your existing software** so CraftFlow pulls in job data, customer history, and dispatch info from tools you already use.

## Who uses CraftFlow?

| Role | How they use CraftFlow |
|------|------------------------|
| **Owners & GMs** | Track team performance, spot revenue trends, review call scoring |
| **Call Center Managers** | Monitor CSR performance, review flagged calls, assign coaching |
| **Service Managers** | Review field tech recordings, assign ride-alongs, track checkpoints |
| **Technicians** | Record calls via the mobile app, view their own feedback |
| **CSRs** | See their scorecard results and coaching assignments |
