---
title: "Getting Started"
description: "Set up CraftFlow for your team in a few steps."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your field management software">
    CraftFlow integrates with ServiceTitan, HubSpot, and other platforms to pull in customer and job data automatically.

    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the app and follow the connection guide for your software.

    <Card title="View integration guides" icon="plug" href="/help/integrations/overview" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Add your call center managers, service managers, and technicians.

    Go to **Settings → Team** and invite members by email. Assign the right role for each person so they only see what's relevant to them.

    <Card title="Team management guide" icon="users" href="/help/account/team-management" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your scorecards">
    Scorecards define what a good call looks like for your business. Build one for your CSRs (inbound booking calls) and one for your technicians (sales calls in the field).

    Go to **Call Center AI → Scorecards** to create your first scorecard.

    <Card title="Scorecard setup guide" icon="clipboard-check" href="/help/call-center-ai/scorecards" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the mobile app">
    Technicians record field calls using the CraftFlow mobile app. Have each tech download the app and sign in with their CraftFlow credentials.

    Available on iOS and Android.

    <Card title="Mobile recording guide" icon="mobile" href="/help/call-recording/mobile-recording" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your first calls">
    Once calls start coming in, head to **Call Center AI → Calls** or **AI Ridealong → Recordings** to see transcripts, scores, and AI insights.

    <Card title="Reviewing recordings" icon="chart-bar" href="/help/call-recording/reviewing-recordings" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Most teams are fully set up within a day. If you run into anything, email [support@craftflow.com](mailto:support@craftflow.com).
</Note>
