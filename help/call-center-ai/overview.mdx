## Agent Coach & QA

Every inbound call is automatically scored against your booking script. No manual QA sampling — Craft listens to every call.

**What you get:**
- **Automatic call scoring** — every call scored, not just a random sample
- **Booking rate by rep** — see who books and who doesn't, and why
- **Spam filtering** — junk calls filtered out so metrics reflect real opportunities
- **Coaching feedback** — CSRs see exactly where they went off-script and what to say differently
- **Loss reason tracking** — understand why calls don't convert (price, timing, service area, competition)

**How it connects:**
Craft integrates with your existing phone system (RingCentral, Five9, or other VoIP/SIP). No hardware, no app downloads for CSRs. Setup takes 1-2 business days during onboarding.
