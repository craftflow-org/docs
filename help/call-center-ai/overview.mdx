---

CraftFlow's **Call Center AI** monitors your inbound call center and gives you visibility into every call — not just the ones you happen to listen to. Every call is transcribed, scored against your scorecards, and surfaced for review if it needs attention.

## What it does

- **Scores every call** automatically using the scorecards you define
- **Flags calls for review** when a score drops below your threshold or a specific issue is detected
- **Tracks revenue recovery opportunities** — calls that could have been booked but weren't
- **Surfaces coaching moments** so managers can address issues quickly

## How calls get into CraftFlow

CraftFlow integrates with your phone system to receive call recordings. Once integrated, calls are processed automatically — no manual uploads required.

<Note>
  Supported integrations include RingCentral and Telnyx. Contact support if you're using a different provider.
</Note>

## The calls list

Go to **Call Center AI → Calls** to see all your inbound calls. Each call shows:

- Date, time, and duration
- CSR name
- Scorecard result (pass/fail and score)
- Any flags or tags applied
